# Statistical and Charting Decision Rules

## Unit of Analysis

The primary unit is the PubMed-indexed record. Companion publications from the same programme are not collapsed into one programme-level unit in the main matrices.

## Evidence-Map Scope

Records were eligible for the broad evidence map when they described HPV vaginal self-sampling or self-collection in under-screened, overdue, never-screened, non-attending, underserved, rural, migrant, minority, or otherwise low-access cervical screening populations and contained sufficient record-level information for charting.

## Implementation-Core Subset

The implementation-core subset includes records with empirical implementation, delivery, uptake, testing, referral, follow-up, equity, resource-use, or programme-evaluation information from real participants or real programmes.

Pure modelling, preference-only, intention-only, qualitative-only, laboratory or assay-performance-only, protocol-only, review, and commentary records without empirical implementation results are retained only as broader contextual records or excluded, depending on eligibility.

## Follow-Up Reporting Levels

- `none`: no visible referral, triage, or follow-up information in the record-level source.
- `referral_only`: referral, invitation, triage, or follow-up mentioned without attendance or completion metrics.
- `completion_reported`: observed follow-up attendance or completion reported as a percentage or numerator/denominator for HPV-positive or otherwise referred participants.

Completion may refer to the next clinical step, including cytology triage, repeat or triage testing, colposcopy, treatment when reported as the next step, or documented diagnostic follow-up. It should not be interpreted as histologically confirmed diagnostic resolution in every record.

## Rounding

Counts are reported as integers. Percentages in text and tables are rounded to one decimal place unless a source record supplied a more specific value used for extraction.

## Interpretation

The outputs are descriptive. No pooled effect estimates, formal risk-of-bias appraisal, certainty-of-evidence grading, time-trend modelling, or multivariable modelling is performed in this release.
